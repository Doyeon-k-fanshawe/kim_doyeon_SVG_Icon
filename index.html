What is the chinese Zodiac?

"Chinese zodiac years are represented by 12 animals. Each Chinese lunar year has a Chinese zodiac sign animal. The Chinese zodiac year's starting date is a little different from the Gregorian year. It starts from the Chinese New Year.

"Originating from China, the zodiac and its variations remain popular in many East Asian and Southeast Asian countries, such as Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, Cambodia, Nepal, Bhutan and Thailand."
